4-Part Inclusive Guide


Our 4-Part Guides are made specific for each field. They compile all the information, statistics, & templates students need to be successful during their undergraduate studies, entrance exams, applications, and interviews.

Record Your Progress


The “Your Progress” tabs allow you to record all your progress throughout your 4 years as an undergraduate student. You can record your hours, planned courses, letters, save chosen schools, update application statuses, and much more.

Extra Services


In the event a student requires additional help with the application/interview process. Guideology offers mock interviews, personal statement & supplemental editing.

1-on-1 Mentoring


Guideology has assigned a team of Guideology has assigned a team of students & advisors dedicated to supporting you throughout this process. Mentors are available to meet with students 1 on 1 to discuss and plan your pathway to dental school.

Student Community


Our forums allow students to interact with one another with the goal of making this process easier & more enjoyable. Students can ask questions, share their experiences, and communicate with one another.

Mobile App


The Guideology app allows you to access our program from your mobile device or tablet. You’ll be able to record your progress, access your guide, student forums, support team, etc. Same as you would on your computer.

We Are Here To Help

At Guideology, we are familiar with the rigorous path one must follow to achieve the goals of attending their top graduate school. We have witnessed the substantial amount of information one must know to succeed in this process, from taking required courses, acing entrance exams, applying, to interviewing, or even setting a timeline and because of that, Guideology was created.

Mission Statement

Our goal at Guideology is to guide you into your dream graduate school. We’ve worked hard to create inclusive and accurate guides that will cover every step of the path you will take towards your preferred career—supplemented by our tracking software, student forums, support team, and extra services, we’ve made that goal possible. We work hard so you and other students can have everything you need all in one place. With an informed staff and a supportive student community we will work hard to overcome this process together.

We are proud to donate 10% of our profits to UNESCO with the goal of helping strengthen education systems worldwide and fighting for the right to education.
